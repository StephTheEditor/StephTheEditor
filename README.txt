NOTES
-----
- The site uses Google Fonts (Playfair Display + DM Sans) loaded from the web.
  An internet connection is required for fonts to display correctly.
- The contact form does not currently submit to a backend. To make it functional,
  connect it to a service like Formspree (formspree.io) — just replace the
  form's onsubmit handler with a Formspree endpoint.
- The IMDb link points to imdb.com — update it with your direct profile URL.
- All awards/nominations listed are real and sourced from public records.
  Placeholder awards rows can be updated or removed as needed.
