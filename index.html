About

Stephanie Erickson is a Creative Editor and Story Architect with credits spanning HBO, HGTV, ABC, Discovery, Roku, MTV, YouTube Originals, and Snapchat. She has built one of the most versatile editorial careers in unscripted television by staying ahead of where the medium is going, not where it has been.

Her signature is rhythmic precision. Whether shaping a competition format, building a docuseries from the ground up, or crafting vertical content for emerging platforms, she delivers the fast-paced, music-driven storytelling that holds modern audiences. A graduate of the School of Film and Television at Loyola Marymount University, she approaches every project as a storyteller first.

Press

The shows Stephanie edits tend to get noticed. Face Off ran for thirteen seasons on Syfy — Entertainment Weekly called it "freakishly entertaining" and TV Guide's Matt Roush declared "the creativity is astounding." Barbie Dreamhouse Challenge drew 10.2 million viewers. Instant Influencer with James Charles pulled 53 million views in its first month and took home the Streamy for Show of the Year. And when Screen Rant reviewed Bachelor Mansion Takeover in 2026, they called it the best Bachelor spin-off ever made. She is not keeping score. The work is.
