About

Stephanie edits television. She produces verticals and leads the creative edit. She tells stories that people cannot stop watching.

She edits across competition, docuseries, and emerging formats. Shaping story through rhythm, behavior, and structure.

Meeting audiences where they are, without flattening the story to fit the format. She is currently focused on scripted vertical formats and narrative long form work alongside her unscripted practice. A graduate of Loyola Marymount University, School of Film and Television. Based in Los Angeles.
