About

Stephanie Erickson is a Creative Editor and Story Architect specializing in the world of prestige streaming and the emerging frontier of platform native vertical content. With an expansive portfolio that includes HBO, Discovery, HGTV, ABC, Roku and MTV, she has built one of the most versatile editorial careers in the industry by constantly staying at the forefront of shifting visual languages.

Her range is her signature. Whether shaping a long form docuseries, building the story architecture of a competition series, or crafting a vertical format from the ground up, Stephanie brings a signature rhythmic precision to every project. She pairs deep technical mastery with an intuitive sense of the now, delivering the fast paced, music driven storytelling that modern audiences demand.

A graduate of the School of Film and Television at Loyola Marymount University, she approaches every edit as a storyteller first. Her work has been recognized with a Streamy Award and a Critics Choice Award win, alongside nominations from the Directors Guild of America and the Television Critics Association.

Press & Awards

Stephanie edits shows that win. Instant Influencer with James Charles won the Streamy Award for Show of the Year at the 10th Annual Streamy Awards in 2020, drawing over 53 million views in its first month alone. Bachelor Mansion Takeover was declared the best Bachelor spin-off series ever produced by Screen Rant upon its 2026 premiere on HGTV.
